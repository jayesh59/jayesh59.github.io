Jayesh Nagpal

I am a PhD student at AAIR Lab, Arizona State University, working with Dr. Siddharth Srivastava. My research focuses on building generalizable models for robotics. I have done my undergraduate in Mechanical Engineering from Maharaja Agrasen Institute of Technology, Delhi, India.
